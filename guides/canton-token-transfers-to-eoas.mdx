---
title: "Canton token transfers to EOAs"
---

Canton transfers are now **1-step by default** on Mainnet. Tokens transfer directly to the recipient with no separate acceptance step.

When receiving tokens to your EOA, you should disable the auto-accept utility token transfer features in your wallet to avoid conflicts with the 1-step transfer flow.
