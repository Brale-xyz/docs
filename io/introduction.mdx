---
title: 'IO Introduction'
description: 'Fund stablecoins through Brale IO in a few quick steps.'
---

Brale IO makes it easy to fund stablecoins and move assets. Follow these quick steps to go from signup to funded stablecoins.

<Steps>
<Step title="Create your IO account">
  Sign up at [app.brale.io/signup](https://app.brale.io/signup). Verify your email (or Google) with a 6-digit code, select your state, and complete identity checks.

  <Columns cols={2}>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/enter-verification-code.png" alt="Enter verification code" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '360px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/state-selection.png" alt="Select your state" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '360px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
  </Columns>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a stablecoin and chain">
  On the dashboard, pick the stablecoin you want to mint and the chain to receive it.

  <Columns cols={2}>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/select-a-stablecoin.png" alt="Select stablecoin" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '360px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/select-a-chain.png" alt="Select chain" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '360px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
  </Columns>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your destination wallet">
  Connect the wallet that will receive the minted stablecoins. Use the Connect flow to pick from supported wallets.

  <Columns cols={3}>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/selected-chain.png" alt="Chain selected" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/connect-a-wallet.png" alt="Connect a wallet" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/connected-wallet.png" alt="Connected wallet" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
  </Columns>
</Step>

<Step title="Fund and confirm">
  Pick the source chain holding your USDC, review the details, and continue. Confirm to mint and transfer the stablecoins.

  <Columns cols={3}>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/continue-funding-stablecoin.png" alt="Continue to fund" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/funding-chain-selected.png" alt="Select funding chain" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/confirm-stablecoin-order.png" alt="Confirm order" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
  </Columns>
</Step>

<Step title="Sign and track status">
  Sign the transaction in your connected wallet. Track status in IO and review recent activity.

  <Columns cols={3}>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/sign-transaction.png" alt="Sign transaction" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/transaction-complete.png" alt="Order status" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
    <Card>
      <img src="/images/brale/io/activity-tab.png" alt="Activity tab" style={{ width: '100%', maxWidth: '320px', borderRadius: '8px' }} />
    </Card>
  </Columns>
</Step>
</Steps>
